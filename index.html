Short CV

I am R&D Lead at Criva AI and a Senior Researcher in Computer Vision and Robotics with Vision for Robotics Lab at ETH Zurich. I finished my Ph.D. in Computer Vision for Aerial Robots at the same laboratory in 2019. I was previously a research fellow in Computer Vision and Machine Learning at the Intelligent Systems Research Laboratory, University of Reading, and before at Augmented Reality and Digital Interactivity Group / Tecgraf, Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro. 

Main interests: 3D Reconstruction; Scene Understanting; Machine Learning; Deep Learning; SLAM; Camera Calibration; and Robotics.
